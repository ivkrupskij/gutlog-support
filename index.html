Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data private?
Yes. Your privacy is our top priority. All data you enter into GutLog is stored exclusively on your device and is never sent to our servers. We have no access to your health information.

How do I export my data?
You can export your logs as a CSV file from the Settings screen. This file can then be shared with your doctor or saved for your records.
